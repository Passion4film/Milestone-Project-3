Welcome to the BitterSweet Chocolate Recipe Sharing Site!

BitterSweet© is a new Chocolate Company which offers fairtrade, ethically sourced, sustainable chocolate. Not only that...but it's delicious!
This website is a place where anybody can create an account and share their wonderful chocolate inspired recipes. A registered user can view all recipes on the site and add/edit/delete their own recipes to their hearts content. ❤️
And while perusing some of the most mouthwatering chocolate recipes on the web - please consider using BitterSweet© chocolate in your baking - the results will be sweet!
BitterSweet© are dedicated to making delicious Vegan chocolate bars, these are a great alternative to chocolate with dairy and can be used in recipes to make your creation a Vegan sensation!
BitterSweet© also have the traditional dairy chocolate bars in a variety of flavours - all our chocolate is made with love!
BitterSweet© comes in dark, milk and white chocolate varients. Try all of them in your chocolate recipes!
