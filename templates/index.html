Welcome to the BitterSweet Chocolate Recipe Sharing Site!

BitterSweet© is a new Chocolate Company which offers fairtrade, ethically sourced, sustainable chocolate.

Not only that...but it's delicious!

This website is a place where anybody can create an account and share their wonderful, chocolate inspired, recipes. Anyone can view all recipes on the site, however only a registered user can add/edit/delete their own recipes to their hearts content. ❤️

And while perusing some of the most mouthwatering chocolate recipes on the web - please consider using BitterSweet© chocolate in your baking - the results will be sweet!

Did you know...

"The history of chocolate can be traced to the ancient Mayans, and even earlier to the ancient Olmecs of southern Mexico. The word chocolate may conjure up images of sweet candy bars and luscious truffles, but the chocolate of today is little like the chocolate of the past. Throughout much of history, chocolate was a revered but bitter beverage, not a sweet, edible treat".
Which is how BitterSweet© got it's name.

For more information on the history of chocolate check out this site here.

BitterSweet© are dedicated to making delicious Vegan chocolate bars, these are a great alternative to chocolate with dairy and can be used in recipes to make your creation a Vegan sensation!

BitterSweet© also have the traditional dairy chocolate bars in a variety of flavours - all our chocolate is made with love!

BitterSweet© comes in white, milk and dark chocolate varients. Try all of them in your chocolate recipes!
